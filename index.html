I'm a 2nd Year BSc Honours undergraduate in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Moratuwa — one of Asia's leading engineering universities. My goal is to become a Machine Learning Engineer, building intelligent systems that solve real problems through data.

Beyond the terminal, I lead photography as Head of Photography Pillar at MoraSpirit and Assistant Pillar Head at FIT Moments. I'm also a web developer, working on projects like the Mora Hiking Club official site using Next.js and Tailwind CSS.

I'm driven by curiosity — whether that's debugging a neural network at 2am or finding the perfect composition on a misty mountain trail in Badulla.
